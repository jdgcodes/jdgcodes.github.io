Liquor & Legos

Tagline "Paint and Sip, for Dudes"
Description Have you ever been dragged by your significant other to one of those painting and wine classes?

Are you sick of sitting on an uncomfortable stool for hours while painting some blurry trees and chugging $6 riesling?

Enough is enough.

Liquor & Legos is a new concept where attendees are fed copious amounts of nice liquor while they relive their childhood by playing with legos. Employees on site are there to keep those glasses filled and faciliate optional challenges, like 'who can build the coolest spaceship in 30 minutes?'. Both liquor and legos will be provided.
Progress Not much. I got drunk last week and ordered some legos on Amazon. But that's it.
