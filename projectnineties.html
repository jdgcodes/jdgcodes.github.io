90s Town

Tagline "The Renaissance Faire for Millenials"
Description If you've ever been to a Renaissance Faire you know it's fun for an hour or two while you laugh at the silly costumes, chow down on a huge turkey leg, and whip a plastic sword around.

Now, imagine a similar fair, but instead of medieval times, it's the 90s!

When you show up at 90s Town, you leave your phones at the door. But don't worry, we have dial-up AOL connections for all those electronic mails. 90s Town has all your favorite 90s activities - Crossfire. Pacman. Don't Wake Daddy. Super Soakers. And many more!

90s Town also hosts special live peformances of your favorite washed up 90s bands.

So travel back in time and come rock out with Eve 6 @ 90s Town!
Progress I spent 10 years of my life living in the 90s so I'm ready to go once funding arrives.
