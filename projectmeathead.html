Meatheads

Tagline "A Gym with a Butcher Shop"
Description It's 6:30pm. You just finished a hard workout at the gym. You're tired & hungry, but the only foods available at the gym are disgusting protein bars and chalky smoothies.

What if there was a gym that healthy and delicous food options for after your workout?

Introducting Meatheads, the gym with a butcher shop. Swing by the shop after you're done working out and grab a fresh t-bone steak to throw on the grill when you get home. Don't have a grill? No problem, we'll cook it for you any way you like it in store.

Meatheads...mediocre idea, fantastic name.
